Beginnings

A scratch guide I wrote three days before v6.41. I intended this to be the first Alt-Tab guide to DotA

By MegfromHercules

September 10th, 2014

[ size=(1-7)]Morning Tavern:[/size]

Heroes to play: Vengeful, Enchantress, Morphling, Syllabear, Crystal Maiden.
Special Heroes: Jugg, Raigor, Lina, Zeus, Sven
Heroes not to play: SA and Naga

[b] Vengeful Spirit (VS) [/b]

Skill build: Max Missle by level 7, get Ultimate at 6, 11, and 16. Stick Stats in early until level 12, then get command aura.

Item build: Get Tangoes early, about 8, do not get RoR. Get extremely cheap items such as bracers and circlets. Your ideal slots by level 7 should be Boots, Tangoes, 2 circs and 2 bracers, or some variation such as clarities or TP. Build up to Manta, and use it after ult.

Items not to get: Stygian, Linkens, Bashers, Malestrom

Strategy: Stun means you will be team's primary ganker. Carry TP so you can keep your enemy on their toes by TPing to different lanes and TPing to heal and immeadiately to another lane. Initiate battle with your ult and manta. Stun dangerous heroes with disables or crazy ult such as CM or Enigma first.

Guide Recommendation: http://forums.dota-allstars.com/index.php?act=ST&f=269&t=100674&st=0#entry1146494

Enchantress

Skill build: Max Impetus by level 7, get ultimate whenever you feel physical attacks will be stronger than nukes. (Usually around midgame or level 10/11) Get one level of heal, then get more if your team needs to push early. (It shouldn’t) Stick in the rest with stats.

Carry Build: Tangoes+Clarities+circs, build to boots and midas, do not get bracers. Get relic and get radiance or dr. aegis. Get Guinsoo if your team needs. Only get BoT

Classic: Bracers+BoT rush. Get Guinsoo.

Items not to get: Orbs. Battlefury. Blink.

Strategy: Powerful orb and range means solo. Use orb excessively, but never to farm like Silencer or Clinkz. You should get wards if mid so you can harass while their hero is being pussy. Avoid ganks, carry a TP, do not gank early, you have no real nukes. You will be powerful mid-game if you are farmed. In team battles, have heal on and sit and kill their Int heroes with your Arrow Effect.

Guide Recommendation: http://forums.dota-allstars.com/index.php?showtopic=116378

Morphling

Skill Build: Waveform whenever you can, max by 7. Stats in between. Don’t get morph unless you have some very pressing issue. Adaptive strike after level 11 or 12 or so, don’t hold off past level 16.

Item Build: Bracer and Wraith then BoT rush. If you aren’t farming well, get SY, if you are, get Bfly.

Items not to get: Stygian, Malestrom, Heart, MKB/Buriza

Strategy: Farm up, try to pair up with a disabler hero. Use waveform to kill and chase. Simple hero to use really. Team battles consist of waveforming and dps.

Guide Recommendation: http://forums.dota-allstars.com/index.php?showtopic=48496

Syllabear

Skill Build: Spirit bear 1,3,5,7—Rabid 4,8,11,12----Synergy 2,6,9,10, True Form if you desperately need a tank at 16,17,18.

Item Build: Midas to Radiance. Treads on bear and BoT with headdress on you.

Items not to get: Bashers, Hyper, Orbs on bear.

Strategy: Use bear to harass easily, let it regen on your headdress. Use it search out forests and do maphack. Control the heroes separately if possible. Solo. Do not rely on bear for dps, Euls is a hard counter. Use bear to last hit easily. Try to initiate battles with your bear.

Guide Recommendation: http://forums.dota-allstars.com/index.php?showtopic=84950

Crystal Maiden

Skill Build: Frost bite at 1, then alternate nova-bite. Get ult at 10 or so, or whenever you get Euls. You need the mana.

Item Build: Bracers or skip them if you can get orge axe. Blink, euls

Items not to get: Aganhims, Damage

Strategy: Do not overrely on your ult. BKB is helpful, but only in team battles. Blink+Bite+Cyclone is usually good enough to take out a few disables for you to ult. If not, get BKB, I am not saying it’s a bad item, but don’t overrely on it. Harass in lane, and gank when you have blink. Do not solo. Read MasterJoe’s guide.

Guide recommendation: http://forums.dota-allstars.com/index.php?showtopic=91239

Special Heroes and what to do:

[list][*]Jugg: Bladefury and Facch is the only reason he is here. Play him aggressively and using omnislash takes intuition.[*]Raigor: Get Blink and chain your combo, then get focused and die. Takes skill to make use of blink beyond this, and to fissure correctly[*]Lina: Carry build is different from mek-guinsoo build. Read Premium Guide for further info. Use nukes to maintain control of lane.[*]Sven: Must farm well, and will be the team’s carry. Easy to use, but might lose effectiveness if proper counters.

Heroes not to get:

[list][*]SA: Wards and no lane control.[*]Naga: Pubstomp hero. Fairly useless in my mind until someone comes with a new strat. Don’t say using ult and then raxing.

[size=(7)]Sunrise Tavern[/size]

Heroes to play: Tinker, Silencer, Furion, Enigma, Chen
Special Heroes: Tiny, Kotl, Tree
Heroes not to play: Ursa, Orge Magi, Techies, PL

[b]Tinker[/b]

Skill Build: Get Missle maxed by 7. Get Laser with it, maxed by 8. Get Rearm whenever you have the mana to support it or you have BoTs. Usually around 11 or so, and you have your items. Get March when you have a Eul’s and can teleport around the map many times without going back to base. Around this time, creep waves are getting bigger too. (Around level 14-17) Level up Rearm when you have Guinsoo’s because 350 mana is not a joke.

Item Build: I suggest Eul’s because it allows you to rearm while heroes are cycloned, and it is very powerful as a first item. However, rearm allows you to have infinite mana with BoT, so if you are farming well, rush to BoT.

Items not to get: Damage, Necrobook, BKB, Arcane Ring, Dagon

Strategy: Play aggressively in lane, most heroes will not be able to deal with your nukes. Your animation is decent for last hitting and harassing and you have good range so lane control should be easy. Play aggressively with nukes and use Clarities and Tangoes. Most heroes cannot deal with your nukes. Try and be paired with a disabler. I am not a great Tinker, so if anybody wants to add to this, please feel free to post.

Guide Recommendation: None as of yet.


[b]Silencer[/b]

Skill Build: Glaives-stats to level 10, get Global Silence at 10, and max out last word after.

Item Build: Try a few bracers then rush to BoT. You can get a Midas if and only if you are the carry and can farm it reasonably. Otherwise finish BoT and get guinsoo. Finish the build with a butterfly or hyper. Get a mek only if you started with a RoR, and there are no other heroes to get Mek. (Chen) I am not a fan of Mek stack.

Items not to get: Dagon, Nulls, Treads

Strategy: Harass with your Orb (manual cast) and orb walk at level 7. Play with Tangoes, because you will need the quick regen instead of a RoR. TP around and farm with the BoT. Play as dps in team battles, and initiate them with Global Silence. Farm with your orb, it helps last hitting a great deal. Concentrate on weaker heroes during team battles and position yourself so heroes are under last word.

Guide Recommendation: http://forums.dota-allstars.com/index.php?showtopic=70496


[b]Enigma[/b]

Skill Build: Max Malefice by 7. Get two levels of stats in between and your ult. Finish Midnight pulse from 8,9,10,11. Don’t level your ult in most cases.

Item Build: BKB, BoT, Aegis. You have good str growth, but get bracers if you are dying a lot. I suggest just saving 1000 for the Orge axe.

Items not to get: Dagon and everything else basically. Blink is useful, but not as useful as one would think on paper.

Strategy: BKB-Midnight Pulse-Black hole is all you need to do in team battles. Malefice stragglers. Pair up with a disabler hero in lane and rake in kills early. Malefice is very strong in ganks. Don’t go mid. Initiate battles with Black Hole. Use Black Hole more often, I see too many noobs try and “save” it for a 5v5 team battle, use it if you think you can get a kill or two during a gank.

Guide Recommendation: http://forums.dota-allstars.com/index.php?showtopic=68803

[b]Furion[/b]

Skill Build: Tele-Sprout-stats-stats-stats-ult- tele x3, then stats the rest of the way.

Item Build: Midas to Dr. Aegis. Manta if you are having trouble farming. Guinsoo’s if you are not the carry.

Items not to get: Stygian, Dagon, Agahnims

Strategy: Farm and solo if possible. Run around ganking with tele-sprout. You should farm until level 7 or so in lane. Don’t gank until that time. Be the primary DPS of the team. Cast Ult whenever you can. Explore the map before the game starts if its –rd or –lm.

Guide Recommendation: None that use this build, which I think is the best.

[b]Chen[/b]

Skill Build: Test of Faith-Pentinence-persuasionx3-ult-persuasion-pent x3-Don’t finish Test of Faith.

Item Build: Mek to Guinsoo or Necrobook if you are feeling bold.

Items not to get: Stygian and Dagon

Strategy: With the recent nerfs, concentrate on either stunners or Satyrs. Satyrs are monsters at pushing with the shockwave. Centaurs are better than Furblogs. Get a troll priest early if you can find one for the heal. Use Test of faith to tele heroes back to base. 6 secs cast and 3 secs cast on ToF is the essentially the same. Practice Microing the creeps to stun and to use shockwave. Pentinence their weakest hero so your team can take them out. Gank repeatedly throughout the map. Watch out for people who ward creep spots. Buy a shitload of sentries.

Guide Recommendation: http://forums.dota-allstars.com/index.php?showtopic=114092

Special Heroes and what to do:

[list][*]Tiny: Avalance and Toss take some degree of skill. Does not suit the metagame very well anymore, with no lane control and low armor. Grow is used to later carry with Butterfly.[*] KotL: Illuminate and Chakra, and who to use mana leak on. Takes some practice to use your nuke. Must have a good team for this hero to be useful. Do not emphasize pushing TOO much. I see this too much.[*] Tree: When to use ult. [/list]

Heroes not to play:

[list][*]Ursa: Solo hero killer. 5 hits and he’s useless.[*] Orge Magi: Higher Level play has no time for randomness.[*] Techies: See above. Rapier aegis only works in pubs.[*]PL: Dopple is good, but counterable. Must farm well, will not be strong in non-em at all. Horrible duration on nuke.[/list]

[size=(7)]Dawn Tavern[/size]

Heroes to play: Beastmaster, Jakrio/THD
Special heroes: PotM
Heroes not to play: Alchemist

[b]Beastmaster[/b]

Skill Build: Axes until level 7, squeeze in one level of summons, and get ult whenever possible. Get Beast Rage if you need to deal more damage in team battles, but not too early.

Item Build: I really like Radiance on this hero, but some people like going Necrobook. Which one you choose is personal preference and depends on your matchup. Beastmaster is a pretty flexible hero, and can be good with many items.

Items not to get: Mask of Madness, SY

Strategy: Early on, try and focus on using axes and harassing with them. If you can get a lanemate with Basilus, then do it. When you hit level 6, you can gank easily and use your ult to initiate battle. Use your crow for a mobile ward. Try running it through common spots like forests and rivers to explore. Good usage just takes experience, but remember that your crow is probably one of the best map awareness spells in DotA.

Guide Recommendation: http://forums.dota-allstars.com/index.php?showtopic=57058

[b]Jakrio[/b]

Skill Build: Dual Breath-Stats, get icepath from 8-11, then macropyre after. I find that macropyre isn’t worth the mana early and dual breath stats allows one to stay in lane forever.

Item Build: Guinsoos, Necrobook, BoT

Items not to get: Dagon, Hyperstone (I’ve seen this so many times, Auto-fire is horrible)

Strategy: Use dual breath to massacre your lane, and use ice path in team battles. Land a macropyre near tower to stop enemy team from tower hugging. Simple hero to use, I think. Use Eul’s to delay your cooldowns.

Guide Recommendation: http://forums.dota-allstars.com/index.php?showtopic=77970

Special Heroes:
[list][*]PotM: Only has nukes, no late game power. Leap aura is useful but intuition is required to use it.[/list]

Heroes not to play:

[list][*]Alchemist: Farming is not DotA. Pubstomp hero.[/list]


[size=(7)]Light Tavern[/size]

Heroes to play: Rhasta, Sniper, Centaur
Special heroes: Luna, Bristleback, Troll, Drow, Omni, Anti-mage, Davion
Heroes not to play: Panda, Bounty Hunter

[b]Rhasta[/b]

Skill Build: Shackles and Hex, maxed out by 9, along with ult. Get stats from that point onwards. Your nuke is not amazing and should not be gotten. Your mana is too precious

Item Build: Eul’s, then either guinsoos or aganhims. Refresher can be gotten later.

Items not to get: Dagon, Arcane Ring, Mek

Strategy: Use Eul’s to ward trap solo heroes, use shackles with other heroes during ganks. Hex is mana intensive, but should be gotten so that it is maxed when early game is about over and you have your eul’s. Late game, use your wards and refresher to take down towers and raxes. You have three disables so you will be a very high target. Try and stay back while your disables are cooling down and remember to disable their dps and heavy damage output heroes.

Guide Recommendation: http://forums.dota-allstars.com/index.php?showtopic=44813

[b]Sniper[/b]

Skill Build: Take Aim first, and Headshot second, getting ult whenever possible. Skip Scattershot for 17-20.

Item Build: Midas and BoT to Dr. Aegis.

Items not to get: Bashers, SY

Strategy: Early in lane, use your normal attack to harass enemy heroes. Animation cancel so that agro drawn is canceled. Stay in lane and farm like hell. It should be easy with a great animation and headshot. Once you have your damage items, just stay back and pick off heroes from the side, like clinkz.

Guide Recommendation: http://forums.dota-allstars.com/index.php?showtopic=53282

[b]Centuar[/b]

Skill Build: Stomp first, double edge second, forget thorns. Get Ult whenever possible.

Item Build: Dagger to Aegis to Radiance

Items not to get: SY, MKB, Buriza, Heart

Strategy: Stay in lane and farm up your blink dagger. Gank with stomp and double edge. Do not solo, because you will get raped. In team battles, initiate with your blink-stomp and just tank for the rest of your team. Getting an aegis will help balance out that huge HP you have, and Radiance will help in team battles as well. Butterfly is great if you can farm it in place of a Radiance. Basically a very simple hero to use.

Special Heroes:

[list][*]Luna: Low range, not intel based. Powerful ult however. Low amount of lane control.[*] Bristleback: If you are not Swissbeat, you might have trouble. He doesn’t have an instant damage nuke, only a 20% slow, but his tanking abilities are second to none. [*]Troll: Farming is easy, Blind is good, but cannot contribute to team until a level 4 item or one of comparable strength is purchased.[*]Drow: Silence is imba. Orb Effect. Problem is that there are better heroes for her role.[*] Omni: Amazing support. Team needs to be melded around this hero however. Str based. No lane control without mana regen.[*]Anti-mage: Powerhouse past level 8 or so. Will he reach it with sufficient gold?[*]Davion: Easy carry with Dr.Aegis at 16 and his AoE Skadi. Likewise though, can he farm it?[/list]

Heroes not to play:

[list][*]Panda: Centaur[*]Bounty Hunter: No power past level 8.[/list]
