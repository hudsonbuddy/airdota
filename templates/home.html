The Final Carry

A guide to 6.82 carry royale featuring Medusa.

People will try a bunch of different items of Medusa, but there are really only two items that you need to get, and they are the Eye of Skadi and Divine Rapier. No other items give you better value. Double Skadi, double rapier is not a troll build.

6.82 First Thoughts

My thoughts on the 6.82 patch the day after it came out.

Because carries are highlighted, carry v carry fights or fights that have both carries there are the most important engagements in the game now. Small garbage fights matter less. Carries should be harder to kill, so miss chance/disarm comes in here, and bashers are huge for the carry v carry fights.
